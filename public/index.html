Why Families Choose PocketTab

Every money decision is clear, trackable, and easier to agree on.

Ask For Money Clearly

Kids or adults can request what they need, with the amount and reason saved in one place.

Approve In Seconds

Parents and admins can approve, decline, or partially settle requests without back-and-forth chat.

Confirm What Was Paid

When someone sends money, the recipient confirms it so everyone knows exactly where things stand.

Handle Repeats Easily

Set up recurring requests for things like transport, lunches, data, or weekly allowances.

Safe For Every Member

Each person has their own PIN, and parents keep control of the actions that matter most.

See The Full Story

How It Works

Get your household set up in three simple steps.

  1. Step 1: Create Your Household

    Set a household name, invite family members, and let each person create their own profile.

  2. Step 2: Run Daily Money Requests

    Submit requests, approve what makes sense, and keep every decision visible to the whole household.

  3. Step 3: Confirm Payments And Move On

    Mark payments as completed, resolve disputes quickly, and stop arguing about who still owes what.

PocketTab makes shared family money simple: everyone knows what was asked, what was approved, and what was paid.
